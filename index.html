About GCK Lab

Tools Built by a Builder,
for Builders

GCK Lab is an independent micro-SaaS studio focused on building focused, automated web tools for the people who power the modern economy — freelancers, independent creators, and small business owners.

We believe great software doesn't have to be complicated or expensive. Each product we ship is intentionally narrow in scope: it does one thing exceptionally well, runs automatically, and gets out of your way.

Our approach combines modern AI and cloud automation with clean, human-centred design — so the technology handles the complexity while you see simple, actionable results.
