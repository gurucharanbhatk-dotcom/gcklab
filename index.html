About GCK Lab

Tools Built by a Builder,
for Builders

GCK Lab is a web tools studio focused on building focused, automated products for freelancers, independent creators, and small business owners across India and beyond.

We believe great software doesn't have to be complicated or expensive. Each product we ship is intentionally narrow in scope: it does one thing exceptionally well, runs automatically, and gets out of your way.

Our tools are built with modern cloud technology and clean, human-centred design — so the technology handles the complexity while you see simple, actionable results.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GCK Lab and our tools.

What is GCK Lab?
GCK Lab is a web tools studio that builds focused, automated tools for freelancers, creators and small businesses. Our tools help with income tracking, review management, podcast transcription, appointment scheduling and more — all free to start.
Are GCK Lab tools free to use?
Yes — core features of all GCK Lab tools are completely free. Some tools may offer optional premium features in the future, but the essentials will always stay free.
What is IncomeWave and who is it for?
IncomeWave is a free freelancer finance dashboard available at incomewave.gcklab.com. It's built for freelancers and gig workers who have irregular income and need help tracking cash flow, estimating taxes, and managing payments across currencies.
When will the other tools launch?
MeetMind is planned for Q2 2026, ReviewHQ for Q3 2026, and PodScript, BookEasy and ReturnBot are all targeted for 2026. Follow us on social media or reach out via email to be notified when they go live.
How do I contact GCK Lab?

Is my data safe with GCK Lab?
Yes. We take data security seriously. All tools use encrypted connections. We never sell your data to third parties. You can read our full Privacy Policy in the footer.
